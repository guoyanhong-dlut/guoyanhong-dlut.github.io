Biography

Guo received the B.E degree in computer science from Jilin Institute of Technology University, China, in 1999. She received the M.Mc. degree and the Ph.D. degree in Management Science and engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China, in 2003 and 2008, respectively. She was a visiting scholar with the business school, Rutgers University from Sep. 2015 to Aug. 2016. She is currently a professor and head of the FinTech and regulatory innovation research team with School of Economics and Management, Dalian University of Technology.

Her current research interests include market engineering; machine learning method and its application; Large-scale model pretraining and stepwise collaborative reasoning; Domain-specific time series foundation model pretraining and fine-tuning; AI-driven quantitative trading and investment decision algorithms; Distributed situation awareness and collaborative decision-making.

She has published several articles in international academic journals, including Journal of Operations Management (UTD24), European Journal of Operational Research (ABS 4), International Review of Financial Analysis (ABS 3), and Decision Support Systems (ABS 3). She also a Corresponding Member, Big Data Committee, China Computer Federation (CCF); Committee Member, Intelligent Service Committee, Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAAI); Member, Data Science and Knowledge Systems Engineering Committee, Systems Engineering Society of China; Reviewer, National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC); Editorial Board Member, LLM and Embodied Intelligence;
