Biography

Yanhong Guo is a Professor and PhD supervisor at the School of Economics and Management, Dalian University of Technology,and Deputy Director of the Institute of Systems Engineering at the university. In recent years, her research has focused on big data and artificial intelligence technologies, including: pre-training of large language models and multi-step collaborative reasoning; pre-training and fine-tuning of large language models for vertical-domain time series; AI-based quantitative trading and investment decision-making algorithms; and distributed situational awareness and collaborative decision-making. She has led over 20 projects,including the National Key Research and Development Programme project 'Cognitive Decision-Making and Multi-Agent Coordination in Capital Markets'. Her publications have appeared in authoritative international journals such as JOC, EJOR , IJF, IRFA and DSS etc., as well as at top-tier conferences including SIG KDD.
